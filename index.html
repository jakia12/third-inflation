xAI’s first product is Inflection-2, a system with a BYTE of humour

Inflection-2

As of recent developments, xAI, Elon Musk's AI startup, has been working on its own version of ChatGPT called Inflection-2. This conversational AI model, equipped with internet browsing capabilities, can answer questions and engage in discussions, similar to ChatGPT and other text-generating models like Meta's Llama 2. Inflection-2 leverages a knowledge base to provide responses and can search the internet for up-to-date information on various topics. ​ However, Inflection-2 has its limitations. The AI is designed to refuse certain sensitive queries, indicating a degree of ethical filtering. Elon Musk has hinted that Inflection-2's responses might incorporate a touch of humor, distinguishing it from other similar models. The system's ability to blend informative responses with a sense of humor could potentially offer users a unique and engaging conversational experience.t
